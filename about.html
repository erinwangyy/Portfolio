Hi, my name is Yueying (Erin) Wang. I was born in China where my previous major was Landscape Architecture. My route changed in 2017, when I saw photography as my destined pursuit. I got my BFA from RISD in the summer of 2020.

I shoot self-portrait and landscape, behind which it is the ancient desire to express and communicate. I am a collector of sentiments and emotions.

Traveling is my favorite part of life. I took trains around the United States. I usually depart from Providence, RI.

Besides adventuring with a camera, I learned some printmaking and web design.
